About Us

The Felder-Williams Project

My name is Michael Felder, l am currently a Distribution Foreman with the Philadelphia Gas Works. As a foreman, my daily responsibilities involve analyzing complex distribution systems, but my real passion in life is bringing awareness about child abuse.

There is an estimated 3.4 million cases of child abuse reported to Child Protective Services. However, in my case, my abuse went unreported. I am an adult victim of sexual child abuse. That feeling of being victimized as a child left me feeling helpless and isolated. With the many trials and tribulations l encountered in life l was able to find the strength to forgive. I was able to find my voice and rise above any obstacle. I knew that if l could find a way out, l could help others who are like me. So I recently developed "The Felder-Williams Project'.

The project consists of me and a host of other visionaries who share the same passion to aid those suffering from abuse and most importantly the adolescent population.

My vision for this project is for all visionaries involved to bring community awareness regarding child abuse, provide donations to families and children in need, and to offer various resources. But we need your help to accomplish this vision.
