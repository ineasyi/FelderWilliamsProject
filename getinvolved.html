Get Involved

How to you can help our mission!

Everyone can get involved! While your financial donations are important to the growth of the Felder-Williams Project so is your time and your voice. You can help spread awareness and support of this project by sharing our Facebook page on your social networks and even by donating your time during different evetns we will be holding in the community to spread awareness. Your voice is imporant! Oftentimes those who have been abused or are still being abused in any way suffer in silence; it is our goal to break the silence and get people talking again so that they can learn ways to healtyily cope and receive healing while spreading wisdom. You can get involved today by becoming a part of the Felder-Williams Project Team by signing up with your email address for our newsletter and opportunities to lend a helping hand or attend our different events. You can also donate financially if you don't have the time to participate in the events. We bellieve that everyone can get involved in some way or another to make a difference, please help us today and sign up for our newsletter for more opportunities to get involved and make a difference in someone's life today. Thank you!
