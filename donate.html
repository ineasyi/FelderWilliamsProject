How you can help our mission!

Everyone can get involved! While your financial donations are important to the growth of The Felder-Williams Project so is your time and your voice. You can help spread awareness and support of this project by sharing our Facebook and Instagram pages on your social networks. You may also donate your time during different events we will be holding in the community to spread awareness. Your voice is important! Oftentimes those who have been abused or are still being abused in any way suffer in silence; it is our goal to break the silence and get people talking again so that they can learn ways to healthily cope and receive healing while spreading wisdom.

You can get involved today by becoming a part of The Felder-Williams Project Team by signing up with your email address for our newsletter and opportunities to lend a helping hand or attend our different events. You can also donate financially if you don't have the time to participate in the events.

We believe that everyone can get involved in some way or another to make a difference someone's life. Thank you!
