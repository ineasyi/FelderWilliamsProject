Janee Latrice
Janee Latrice is a recording artist who hails from West Philadelphia. A product of military parents and a CAPA High School alum, Latrice values the exposure to international art and culture she has been privileged to experience. Though she stands in a lane all her own, some of her favorite artists to emulate include Jill Scott, Michael Jackson and Amy Winehouse. She was most recently invited to perform in Philadelphia for the Democratic National Convention's Delegates Welcome Reception and consistently takes stages all around her city. Janee is grateful for every opportunity to grace a stage, and prides herself on leaving her supporters with an impactful experience each time!"
Sap Sounds
Sap Sounds makes a conscious effort to construct music compositions and authentic works that express his eclectic, organic artistry with true musicianship. His mission is to lend in the rebirth of genuine, savage, untamed, love in melody. Sap can’t accurately be defined by nor confined to one genre over the other. He is the antithesis of modern music, due to an unorthodox approach to music, diverse influence, and a multicultural upbringing, Sap seamlessly weaves and crosses Hip-Hop, Acoustic Soul, Pop, R&B, and Jazz.
David Lyve
Yes, I’m David Lyve, that’s Lyve with a Y and not an I! As you have assumed correctly, I help lost ears find their good music home, through singing, songwriting, producing, and real instrument playing. But you can just say I am a recording artist if that’s easier. People have turned into fans of my music when they are feeling exhausted of the over computerized gimmicks that rob them of a chance to ‘feel.’I have 10+ years background in singing, songwriting, performing, musicianship, and producing. I’ve shared stages with huge artist, not as huge artist, and many in-between artists. I have an emotional and spiritual perspective on what it takes to pique an audience’s attention and build the obsession.

Chris Cotton
Chris Cotton began performing Stand Up Comedy in the same city he was born and raised in(Philadelphia, Pennsylvania). Chris has worked with many of the best national headliners including Artie Lange, Keith Robinson, Godfrey, Bret Ernst, Tommy Davidson, Kevin Hart, Judah Frielander, and Hannibal Burress.
